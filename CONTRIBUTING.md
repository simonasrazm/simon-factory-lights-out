# Contributing to SFLO

Thanks for your interest in improving the Simon Factory Lights Out protocol.

## How to Contribute

- **Open an issue** — describe the problem, share your experience, or suggest an improvement
- **Start a discussion** — if you've used SFLO with your own agent setup, I'd love to hear what worked and what didn't

## What I'm Looking For

- Improvements to gate definitions based on real-world usage
- New agents that would generally benefit anyone (see `docs/agent-spec.md` for the agent format)
